Privacy Policy

1. Overview

YouBox operates a unified API gateway that routes requests to third-party model providers. This policy explains what we process, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We talk to developers as peers — no dark patterns, no buried clauses.

By default, YouBox does not store the content of your prompts or completions. Pass-through requests honor each provider's zero-data-retention terms.

2. Data we process

To operate the gateway we process account data (email, hashed password, OAuth identifiers), billing data (handled by our payment processors), and request metadata (model, token counts, latency, status code, timestamp). Request metadata powers your dashboard and billing.

  • We do not log prompt or completion text in normal operation.
  • We do not sell personal data, ever.
  • We do not train any model on your traffic.

3. Zero retention

When a provider supports zero data retention, YouBox forwards your request without persisting the body. Aggregated, non-identifying metrics (e.g. tokens routed per day) may be retained to operate and improve the service.

4. Sub-processors

We rely on infrastructure and payment sub-processors to deliver the service, including cloud hosting, our database and cache layer, and Stripe for payments. A current list is available on request and updated before any material change.

5. Security

API keys are stored hashed. Transport is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3). Admin access requires two-factor authentication. We support SSO, SAML, and audit logs on Enterprise plans.

6. Your rights

You may access, export, or delete your account data at any time from the dashboard. Deletion is permanent and removes your keys, history, and personal data within 30 days.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach our team at privacy@youbox.dev. We respond within two business days.
